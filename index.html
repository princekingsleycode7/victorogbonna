Hello There

You are invited to participate in a vital academic research project conducted by Chukwuma Victor Ogbonnaya, a PhD candidate at the European University Cyprus. This study, titled "Developing a Sustainable Leadership Model for Learning Organizations In Nigeria," aims to understand how employee learning preferences and leadership styles can create more effective and adaptable organizations within Nigeria's cassava production industry. Your experience and perspective are invaluable to the success of this research.

Why Your Participation Matters

Your Contribution is Essential

By sharing your insights, you will help:

  • Contribute to a new understanding of leadership in the Nigerian context.
  • Improve Workplace Practices: The findings could lead to better training programs and more supportive leadership models.
  • Shape the Future: Help build a foundation for more sustainable and successful "learning organizations" in your industry.

Privacy and Confidentiality

Your Privacy is Our Priority and We are committed to the highest standards of confidentiality and data protection in line with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

  • Complete Anonymity: You are not required to provide your name or any identifying information.
  • Confidential Data: All responses will be kept strictly confidential and used solely for the purpose of this PhD research.
  • Secure Storage: The data will be stored securely and will be kept only for the period needed for the research, which is expected to be one year after the project's completion.
